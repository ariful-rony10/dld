Meet Our Team

  • DR. MOHAMMAD SHIDUJAMAN

    Supervisor

    Dr. Mohammad Shidujaman is an Assistant Professor in the department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), Faculty of Engineering, American International University- Bangladesh (AIUB), Dhaka, Bangladesh. Mohammad Shidujaman received his Ph.D. degree in Design from Tsinghua University. His doctoral research emphasized developing expressive interaction design techniques for social robots that involve multidisciplinary research in Design for human-robot perceptual interconnection. His research interests include AI, Robotics, Interaction design, IoT, Computational Intelligence, Social and Cultural Anthropology, etc.
